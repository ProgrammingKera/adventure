# AI Trip Planner - Project Requirements

## Backend Dependencies (Node.js)
axios@^1.6.0
cors@^2.8.5
dotenv@^16.3.1
express@^4.18.2
form-data@^4.0.4
multer@^1.4.5-lts.1

## System Requirements
Node.js: v14.0.0 or higher
npm: v6.0.0 or higher
